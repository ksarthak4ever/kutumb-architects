About Me

Who is Jyoti?

Jyoti is a qualified architect having total experience of more than 15 years in architectural field.

She has worked with Architects Firm in UP, Haryana and Uttarakhand in her initial days and later on worked with Godrej and Boyce mfg. co. ltd.

Her goal is to create sustainable habitat for human beings. To create good buildings contains simple shape and terminology in order to create harmony with nature. Her background is creative designing but parallelly competitive approach.

Passions & Aspirations

She is fueled by her passion for understanding human requirement for living and to create a space best for the user as per their respective requirement and liking.

She considers herself as a ‘forever student’ eager to both build on her academic foundation in architecture and related fields like interior designing and landscaping and to stay tuned with the latest strategies and technologies through continued coursework and professional development.

She is always interested in a challenge.
